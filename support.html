Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set the recording threshold?

Use the slider on the main screen to adjust the sensitivity. The white line on the audio meter shows your threshold. Audio above the threshold (red) will be captured; audio below (green) will be skipped.

Why isn't my transcription working?

Make sure you've granted Speech Recognition permission in Settings. Also ensure you have an internet connection, as transcription may require Apple's servers.

Can I use this offline?

Recording works offline. Transcription may require an internet connection depending on your device and iOS settings.

Where are my recordings saved?

Recordings are stored temporarily until you share them. Use the Share Audio button to save the file to your preferred location.
