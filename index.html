Transcription Keeper

Turn your voice into text with one tap. Smart threshold recording captures what matters and skips the silence.

Features

Smart Threshold Recording

Set a sensitivity threshold to automatically skip silence. Only capture audio when it matters - perfect for meetings, lectures, or voice notes.

Instant Transcription

Your audio is transcribed to text as soon as you stop recording. No waiting, no hassle.

Easy Sharing

Share your transcription as text or the original audio file. Send to any app with the built-in share sheet.

Visual Audio Meter

See your audio levels in real-time with a visual meter. Adjust the threshold before recording to get it just right.
