Privacy Policy

Transcription Keeper

Overview

Transcription Keeper is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect no personal data and have no servers that store your information.

Data Collection

Transcription Keeper does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your recordings and transcriptions stay on your device unless you choose to share them.

Audio & Transcription

When you record audio, the file is stored temporarily on your device. Transcription is performed using Apple's Speech Recognition framework, which may process audio on-device or through Apple's servers depending on your device and settings.

We do not have access to your audio recordings or transcriptions. Apple's privacy policy governs how they handle speech recognition data.

Permissions

Transcription Keeper requests the following permissions:

  • Microphone: Required to record audio
  • Speech Recognition: Required to transcribe audio to text

These permissions are used solely for the app's core functionality and nothing else.

Third-Party Services

Transcription Keeper does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

Contact
